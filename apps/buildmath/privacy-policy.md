# BuildMath — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 12, 2026_

BuildMath is built to be private by design. This policy describes what data
the app handles and how.

## The short version

**We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your data.** Everything
you enter in BuildMath stays on your device.

## What data the app handles

- **Calculator inputs** exist only on screen while you use a calculator.
- **App settings** (such as your unit preference and Pro unlock status) are
  stored locally on your device.

BuildMath has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking
SDKs, and no server of its own. The app works fully offline.

## Purchases

The optional one-time Pro unlock is processed by Apple (App Store) or Google
(Google Play). We never see or store your payment details. Payment processing
is governed by Apple's or Google's own privacy policies.

## Data deletion

Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data it stored, because that data
exists only on your device.

## Children

BuildMath does not collect data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices ever change, this page will be updated and the
"Last updated" date revised before the change takes effect.
