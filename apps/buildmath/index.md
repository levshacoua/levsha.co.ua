Buy the right amount of materials the first time.

BuildMath is a set of fast, no-nonsense calculators for renovation and DIY
projects. Type your measurements and get instant quantities — including
sensible waste allowances — so you don't overpay or run short mid-job.

## Calculators

- **Concrete slab** — volume and 25 kg dry-mix bags
- **Paint** — liters or gallons and cans, by area and coats
- **Tile / flooring** — tile count with 10% waste included
- **Wallpaper** — strips and rolls for your room
- **Drywall** — sheets for walls or ceiling
- **Mulch / soil** — volume and bags to cover an area

## Built for the job site

- Results update live as you type — no "calculate" button
- Metric and imperial units, switchable with one tap
- Works fully offline, anywhere
- No account, no ads, no data collection

## Simple pricing

Concrete and paint calculators are free. A single one-time purchase unlocks
all calculators forever — no subscription, no recurring fees.

Measure, type, build. BuildMath does the math.
