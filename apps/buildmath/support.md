# BuildMath — Support

Quick material calculators for renovation and DIY — concrete, paint, tile,
wallpaper, drywall, mulch. Fully offline.

## FAQ

**1. How do the calculators work?**
Open a calculator, type your measurements, and results update instantly below
the inputs. Most calculators include a 10% waste allowance — it is named in
the result row when applied.

**2. How do I switch between metric and imperial units?**
Tap the **m / ft** button in the top bar. The choice applies everywhere and is
remembered.

**3. Why do I see “Enter positive numbers above”?**
One of the inputs is empty, zero, negative, or not a number. Both `.` and `,`
work as a decimal separator.

**4. What does Pro include and how do I restore it?**
Pro is a one-time purchase that unlocks all calculators. If you reinstalled
the app or switched devices, open Settings → Pro and tap **Restore Purchases**
(make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID / Google account).

**5. Are the results guaranteed to be exact?**
They are estimates based on your inputs and common waste factors. Always
double-check quantities before buying materials — see the
[terms of use](/apps/buildmath/terms-of-use/).
