# FreshKeep — Support

Track expiry dates of food, meds and cosmetics — fully offline and private.

## FAQ

**1. How do I add an item?**
Tap the “+” button (top bar on iPhone, round button on Android), enter a name,
pick a category and the expiry date, then tap Save.

**2. Why am I not getting reminders?**
FreshKeep asks for notification permission when you save your first item. If
you declined it, enable notifications for FreshKeep in your system Settings.
Reminders arrive at 9:00 — three days before expiry and on the expiry day.

**3. How do I delete an item?**
Swipe it from right to left. You have a few seconds to tap **Undo** if it was
a mistake.

**4. What does Pro include and how do I restore it?**
Pro is a one-time purchase that removes the 15-item limit. If you reinstalled
the app or switched devices, open Settings → Pro and tap **Restore Purchases**
(make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID / Google account).

**5. Where is my data stored? Do I need an account?**
No account needed. All data is stored only on your device — see the
[privacy policy](/apps/freshkeep/privacy-policy/).
