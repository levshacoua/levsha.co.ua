Never let food spoil or medicine expire unnoticed again.

FreshKeep is a simple, fast tracker for everything in your fridge, pantry,
medicine cabinet, and cosmetics shelf. Add an item in seconds, and FreshKeep
reminds you before it expires — three days ahead and on the day itself.

## Why FreshKeep

- Add an item in under 10 seconds: name, category, date — done
- Clear color badges show what is fresh, expiring soon, or already expired
- Reminders arrive at 9:00, three days before expiry and on expiry day
- Swipe to delete used-up items, with undo
- Four categories: food, meds, cosmetics, other

## Private by design

- No account, no sign-up, works fully offline
- Your data never leaves your device — nothing is collected or shared
- No ads, no tracking, no analytics

## Simple pricing

FreshKeep is free for up to 15 tracked items. A single one-time purchase
unlocks unlimited items forever — no subscription, no recurring fees.

Less waste, fewer surprises, more peace of mind.
