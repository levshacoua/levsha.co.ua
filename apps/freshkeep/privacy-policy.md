# FreshKeep — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 12, 2026_

FreshKeep is built to be private by design. This policy describes what data the
app handles and how.

## The short version

**We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your data.** Everything
you enter in FreshKeep stays on your device.

## What data the app handles

- **Items you track** (names, categories, expiry dates) are stored only in the
  app's local storage on your device. They never leave your device.
- **App settings** (such as your Pro unlock status) are stored locally on your
  device.

FreshKeep has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking
SDKs, and no server of its own. The app works fully offline.

## Notifications

Expiry reminders are **local notifications**, scheduled and delivered entirely
on your device by the operating system. No notification data is sent anywhere.

## Purchases

The optional one-time Pro unlock is processed by Apple (App Store) or Google
(Google Play). We never see or store your payment details. Payment processing
is governed by Apple's or Google's own privacy policies.

## Data deletion

Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data it stored, because that data
exists only on your device.

## Children

FreshKeep does not collect data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices ever change, this page will be updated and the
"Last updated" date revised before the change takes effect.
